# Broadcom Identity & Access Management Resources (Symantec)

Broadcom's Symantec division offers a comprehensive Identity & Access Management (IAM) portfolio, focusing on Zero Trust principles.

## 1. Privileged Access Management (PAM)

Symantec PAM (formerly CA PAM) protects sensitive administrative credentials and controls privileged user access across hybrid environments.

### Key Features
*   **Privileged Credential Vault:** Secure storage and rotation of passwords/keys.
*   **Session Recording:** Real-time monitoring and recording of privileged sessions.
*   **Threat Analytics:** Behavioral analysis to detect compromised accounts.
*   **Secrets Management:** Removes hard-coded credentials from DevOps pipelines.

## 2. Identity Governance and Administration (IGA)

Symantec IGA streamlines the lifecycle management of user identities and their access rights.

### Key Components
*   **Identity Manager:** Automated user provisioning and de-provisioning based on HR events.
*   **Identity Governance:** Access certification, role management, and compliance reporting.
*   **Identity Portal:** A business-user-friendly interface for requesting access and managing approvals.

### Key Features
*   **Access Certification:** Periodic reviews to ensure users only have necessary access.
*   **Policy Enforcement:** Detects and prevents Separation of Duties (SoD) violations.
*   **Visualizer:** Graphical representation of roles and entitlements.
