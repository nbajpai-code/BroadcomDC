# Broadcom Data Center Networking Resources

## 1. Ethernet Switching Chips (StrataXGS and StrataDNX)

Broadcom's Ethernet switching portfolio powers the majority of hyperscale and enterprise data centers.

### Tomahawk Series (High-Bandwidth Hyperscale)
*   **Tomahawk 5 (BCM78900):** 51.2 Tbps bandwidth, 5nm process. High-radix 800G connectivity.
*   **Tomahawk 4 (BCM56990):** 25.6 Tbps bandwidth, 7nm process.
*   **Tomahawk 3 (BCM56980):** 12.8 Tbps bandwidth, 16nm process.
*   **Documentation:** [Tomahawk Series Product Page](https://www.broadcom.com/products/ethernet-connectivity/switching/strataxgs/tomahawk-5-bcm78900)

### Trident Series (Enterprise & Feature-Rich)
*   **Trident 5-X12 (BCM78800):** High-feature enterprise and campus. Programability.
*   **Trident 4 (BCM56880):** Fully programmable pipeline (NPL).
*   **Trident 3 (BCM56870):** Balanced bandwidth and features.
*   **Documentation:** [Trident Series Product Page](https://www.broadcom.com/products/ethernet-connectivity/switching/strataxgs)

### Jericho Series (Routing & Deep Buffers)
*   **Jericho3-AI (BCM88890):** Optimized for AI workloads, lossless fabric.
*   **Jericho2c+ (BCM88820):** Carrier Ethernet & Edge.
*   **Documentation:** [Jericho Series Product Page](https://www.broadcom.com/products/ethernet-connectivity/switching/stratadnx)

## 2. Network Interface Cards (NICs)

### NetXtreme E-Series
*   **Features:** RoCEv2 (RDMA over Converged Ethernet), NVMe-oF, TruFlow offload engine.
*   **Applications:** Virtualization, HCI, Storage Networking.
*   **Documentation:** [Ethernet Network Adapters](https://www.broadcom.com/products/ethernet-connectivity/network-adapters)

## 3. Fibre Channel Networking (Brocade)

Broadcom's Brocade division leads the Fibre Channel market with Gen 7 (64G) technology.

### Gen 7 Directors (X7 Series)
*   **X7-8 Director:** High availability, scalable 14U chassis. Up to 384 64G ports.
*   **X7-4 Director:** Mid-range scalable director.
*   **Key Features:** Autonomous SAN (self-learning, self-optimizing, self-healing).
*   **Documentation:** [Brocade X7 Directors Data Sheet](https://www.broadcom.com/products/fibre-channel-networking/directors/x7-directors)

### Gen 7 Switches
*   **Brocade G730 Switch:** High-density 128-port switch.
*   **Brocade G720 Switch:** Mid-range 64-port switch.

### Gen 6 Switches
*   **Brocade G630 Switch:** High-density Gen 6 (32G).
*   **Brocade G620 Switch:** 64-port, 1U switch supporting 32G.
*   **Brocade G610 Switch:** Entry-level enterprise switch.
*   **Documentation:** [Brocade Fibre Channel Switches](https://www.broadcom.com/products/fibre-channel-networking/switches)

### Fabric OS (FOS)
*   The operating system powering Brocade hardware.
*   **Current Version:** FOS 9.x (supporting Gen 7).
*   **Documentation:** [Fabric OS Administration Guide](https://docs.broadcom.com/doc/fos-91x-admin-guide)
